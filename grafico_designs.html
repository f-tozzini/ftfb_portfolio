Gráfico Designs

the brief:For our Multimedia Authoring class, students were supposed to create a digital agency. We were required to develop a brand, a website and stationary for our new company.

My roles:Front-end Developer, Marketing.

Collaborators:Me, Jennifer Rozman and Katrina Dorsett.

the project:“Gráfico” means graphic in Portuguese. The naming of the agency was the kick-start of its positioning as a multicultural company, to be known globally. The focus of Gráfico is to provide clients and businesses with affordable design and photography. Designs that are stripped down to core needs, with sleek yet simple ideas; and images that convey a strong meaning while still visually striking.
