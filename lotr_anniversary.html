Lord of the Rings 15th Anniversary

the brief:2016 was the 15th anniversary of the launch of the movie “Lord of the Rings, the Fellowship of the Ring”. We were responsible to design and develop a responsive website for the happening. Each student was also tasked to create a promotional video and a teaser, as well as a photo gallery and a contact form.

the project:For this project, I developed a promotional website. By providing a contest with an attractive prize, I hoped to attract the fans to the platforms, as well as to promote engagement with the public and awareness about the anniversary. The website communicates the steps to participate the contest and offers some information about the movie. The user would sign up to the website and record a video answering the question: “What would be the craziest thing I would do for LOTR?” The best video would win a private re-release movie session with all LOTR's cast.
